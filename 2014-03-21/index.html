Short description

The work, made step by step following the exercises in order, represents a simplified model of the Temple of Concordia of Agrigento (Italy). The first exercise is a simple plan of the model from top view: you can see in particular the several columns of the temple and the internal structure. The second exercise is a 2.5D representation of the temple, including north, south, east, west (and top) plans of the model. The third exercise is obviously the most impressive because is not 2.5D but real 3D. I tried to choose colors similar to the real model, defining custom colors in RGB.
I chose this temple because I really like that kind of Greek architecture models.
