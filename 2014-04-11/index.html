Short description

This second homework is a more detailed version of the first one. In the first exercise I improved a bit the Temple, adding some particular details. In the second exercise I added the missing windows on the interior of the Temple: these windows are made with a retangle and a semicircle, differenced with the wall, to create the holes. The third exercise adds a street and some very simple buildings near the Temple. The fourth exercise is absolutely the most complicated: the road now has lamps, lights effects, a bus stop and sidewalk borders. I added also a small road to reach the Temple, including also a stair. There were added also some trees made of cylinders and cones. At last I added a small kiosk to take a break.
